> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is friendly, and everyone is willing to help when needed. The atmosphere is comfortable and positive.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentors provide detailed guidance, explain things clearly whenever I have questions, and always encourage me to ask and learn.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks are relevant to my field of study. I had the opportunity to apply what I learned at university while gaining new knowledge.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
I learned more about AWS, teamwork, task management, and problem-solving skills in a real working environment.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
Everyone in the team is friendly and supportive. This made me feel comfortable and more confident during my internship.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program provides many useful knowledge-sharing sessions. The internship schedule is also flexible.
